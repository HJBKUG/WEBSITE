What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and problem-solve like humans. AI enables computers to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as:

  • Visual perception and recognition
  • Speech and language understanding
  • Decision-making and problem-solving
  • Learning from experience

Types of AI

🧠 Narrow AI

Designed for specific tasks like voice assistants, recommendation systems, and image recognition.

💡 General AI

Hypothetical AI that can perform any intellectual task a human can do. Still in development.

🚀 Super AI

Theoretical AI that surpasses human intelligence in all aspects. A topic of much debate.

🏥

🚗

🎮

Entertainment

Business

History of AI

1950

Alan Turing proposes the Turing Test to measure machine intelligence.

1956

The term "Artificial Intelligence" is coined at the Dartmouth Conference.

1997

IBM's Deep Blue defeats chess champion Garry Kasparov.

2011

IBM Watson wins Jeopardy! against human champions.

2016

Google's AlphaGo defeats world champion Lee Sedol in Go.

2022-Present

AI chatbots and generative AI transform how we interact with technology.
